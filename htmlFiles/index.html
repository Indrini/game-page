My favourite games

Metal Gear Solid

  • Metal Gear Solid was revolutionary for its time. A tactical espionage game with an ambitious and bizarre story with many unexpected twists and turns.
  • Epic boss battles, each unique with captivating mechanics. Each boss has a big personality and engaging backstory. Psycho-Mantis being the highlight - a telekenetic mind-reader who would gague your personality on how often you saved and predict your every move.
  • The soundtrack was amazing and truly iconic, changing with each environment and evoking a rollercoaster of emotions.

Pathfinder: Wrath of the righteous

  • A truly epic and deep role-playing game where your choices really matter.
  • This is a game where you can truly lose yourself for weeks on end. The world is massive and beautiful with lots of attention to detail. Turn-based combat is a wonderful addition and you have the option to switch between real-time with pause and turn-based on the fly.
  • Your companions are well-fleshed out with engaging backstories that make you either love them or hate them. The crusade system is a step-up from the kingdom management system in the previous game, though this is not for everyone and thankfully you can let the game handle the crusade for you.

Divinity:Original Sin 2

  • A turn-based role playing game with a vast world that is a delight to explore. It offers a unique co-operative experience.
  • Clever, complex combat that allows you to use your environment to your advantage.
  • You can steal your way to riches, robbing merchants blind while one of your other characters distracts them. Interactivity like no other game - if you can imagine it, the game probably allows you to do it!
