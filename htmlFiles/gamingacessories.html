Must-have PC Gaming Accessories

Curved Gaming Monitor

  • Makes your gaming experience more immersive
  • freesync or gsync support with a high refresh rate and low response timecan limit screen-tearing and reduce lag.
  • More comfortable for your eyes and reduces eye strain so you can game for longer!

Back-lit Mechanical Keyboard

  • Mechanical keys allow for faster typing, you will be able to move your character much faster and with less finger fatigue
  • Customisable macros to automate tasks in games. Multiple profiles can be set-up for different games
  • Backlights are cool! You can add a nice style to your gaming set up and also play in the dark!

RGB Gaming Mouse

  • Will make your gaming experience more comfortable with their ergonomic designs
  • Extra mouse buttons you can map to certain keys on the keyboard or to perform macro tasks.
  • Improve performance in games and have better control with customisable DPI (dots per inch)
